30 second Gameplay description [DG]
the player controls a character holding a bowl .you catch shapes falling from top to bottom. 
You move by pressing buttons to move and dash around the screen.
 The default keybinds are left/right arrows to move and shift to dash.

Features Points [DG]
Able to switch skin in built in skin maker. Design own falling shapes and catcher.
Shapes with a red outline, are special shapes that will appear to help you catch the next shape when dashing is considered
impossible to catch it in a strict timespan allocated. Collecting a outlined shape will make the catcher's dash fast enough 
to catch the next shape.

Market Analysis [DG]
Compared to Fruit Ninja. And other catching games.
Aims to make money is with a small price of 4.99$
