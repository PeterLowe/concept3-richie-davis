Concept Document for a Catch Game (Richie, Davis)

Elevator pitch [RB]
-
This Catch game captures the good old feeling of catch that we all know and love.
But this time, you can have your own spin and feeling to it while you clear or create levels and play against your friends,
with your very own skins and sounds you can create and change that creates huge longevity.

World Background [RB]
-
The main world background exists with our newly improved engine. 
Base game has a retro style with shapes (circles, squares, triangles, etc) with neon lights.
You can also create and change your own catch game.

Pillars [RB]
-
Core concepts and themes of our game include the original retro style with neon lights, shapes, and funky retro music.
